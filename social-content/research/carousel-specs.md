# Platform Specs & Best Practices — 2025/2026

## LinkedIn Carousels

### Upload specs

- **Dimensions**: 1080 × 1350 px (4:5 portrait) — fills mobile feed, most real estate
- **Alt option**: 1920 × 1080 px (16:9 landscape) — fewer impressions but desktop-friendly
- **Format**: PDF (multi-page) — LinkedIn renders each page as a swipeable slide
- **Max pages**: 30 (sweet spot: 7–12 for engagement, 5–7 for TOFU)
- **Max file size**: 100 MB (keep under 5 MB for fast load)
- **Font size minimum**: 24 pt body, 36 pt headline (legible on small screens)

### Engagement mechanics

- **Hook slide** (slide 1): Must stop the scroll in <1 second. Use: contrarian claim, data shock, curiosity gap, or bold question
- **Swipe rate drops 40% after slide 3** — front-load value, escalate tension through middle slides
- **CTA slide** (last): Clear single action — "Follow for more", "Drop your answer below", "Save this post"
- **Carousels get 2–3× more reach** than single images, 1.5× more than text-only posts
- **Saves > shares > comments > likes** for algorithm weight (saves signal "reference value")

### Timing (security/tech audience)

- **Best days**: Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday
- **Best times**: 7–9 AM local, 12–1 PM, 5–6 PM
- **Frequency**: 3–5 posts/week, 1–2 carousels/week

### Topics that perform in cybersecurity niche

1. **Myth-busting** — "MFA is not enough" / "SOC analysts don't code"
2. **Frameworks** — MITRE ATT&CK explainers, Cyber Kill Chain breakdowns
3. **Tool comparisons** — SIEM vs EDR vs XDR, open-source vs commercial
4. **Career advice** — "How I broke into DFIR" / "Skills that matter in 2026"
5. **Incident anatomy** — anonymized attack chain walkthroughs
6. **Hot takes** — "Alert fatigue is a management problem, not a tool problem"
7. **Statistical shock** — "80% of breaches use valid credentials"
8. **How-to guides** — "Build a home lab in 30 minutes"

## Instagram

### Carousel/Reel specs

- **Square carousel**: 1080 × 1080 px (1:1)
- **Portrait carousel**: 1080 × 1350 px (4:5) — preferred, fills more screen
- **Story/Reel**: 1080 × 1920 px (9:16)
- **Max carousel slides**: 10 (hard limit)
- **Format**: JPG or PNG per slide
- **Font size minimum**: 28 pt body, 48 pt headline

### Engagement

- **Instagram favors Reels** over carousels in reach (2:1)
- **Carousels get 1.4× more reach** than single posts
- **Saves are king** — algorithm treats saves as "high quality" signal
- **Hashtags**: 3–5 relevant (not 30), mixed size (1 large + 2 medium + 2 niche)

### Cybersecurity content on Instagram

- Visual-heavy infographics
- "Swipe to learn" tutorials
- Stat graphics with dark backgrounds (cyber aesthetic)
- Reels: screen recordings of tools, quick tips

## Twitter/X

### Specs

- **Character limit**: 280 (free), 25,000 (Premium)
- **Thread**: Chain of tweets, each ≤280 chars
- **Optimal thread length**: 5–12 tweets for depth without drop-off
- **Images**: 1600 × 900 px (16:9) recommended, up to 4 images/tweet
- **Polls**: 2–4 options, 1–7 days duration

### Engagement

- **Hook tweet** (tweet 1): Must be standalone valuable. Thread-readers drop 50% by tweet 4
- **Quote tweets > retweets** for amplification
- **Threads get 63% more impressions** than single tweets (when done well)
- **Best times**: 8–10 AM, 12–1 PM weekdays

### Cybersecurity Twitter (InfoSec Twitter)

- **Hot takes** go viral — controversial opinions get quote-tweeted
- **IOCs and detection rules** get saved and shared
- **"Thread 🧵" format**: Hook → context → points → conclusion → CTA
- **Screenshots of terminal output** get high engagement
- **Tag relevant researchers** for amplification
