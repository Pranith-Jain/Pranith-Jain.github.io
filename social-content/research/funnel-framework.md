# Funnel Framework — TOFU / MOFU / BOFU Content Mapping

## Why a funnel?

Most cybersecurity creators post random content and wonder why they don't grow.
A funnel ensures every post has a PURPOSE and moves the audience toward a GOAL.

```
TOFU (Top of Funnel)     → Attract strangers → Awareness
MOFU (Middle of Funnel)  → Nurture followers → Consideration
BOFU (Bottom of Funnel)  → Convert fans     → Decision
```

---

## TOFU — Top of Funnel (Awareness)

**Goal**: Maximize reach. Get strangers to stop, read, and follow.

**Audience**: Cold. Never heard of you. Scrolling mindlessly.

**Content types**:
| Type | Example | Platform |
|------|---------|----------|
| Myth-bust carousel | "5 MFA myths that will blow your mind" | LinkedIn, IG |
| Stat shock graphic | "80% of breaches use valid creds" | IG, Twitter |
| Hot take thread | "Unpopular pentest opinion" | Twitter |
| Infographic | "The Cyber Kill Chain in 60 seconds" | LinkedIn, IG |
| Curiosity gap | "The one detection rule you're missing" | LinkedIn |
| "Did you know" | "DNS is the #1 covert channel. Here's proof." | All |

**Metrics that matter**: Impressions, follows, shares, saves

**Post frequency**: 60% of your content mix

**Funnel position**: Wide top. Cast a wide net. Volume matters.

---

## MOFU — Middle of Funnel (Consideration)

**Goal**: Build authority. Turn followers into engaged fans.

**Audience**: Warm. Follows you. Reads your stuff. Considering trusting you.

**Content types**:
| Type | Example | Platform |
|------|---------|----------|
| Tool comparison | "SIEM vs EDR vs XDR — when to use each" | LinkedIn carousel |
| Framework deep-dive | "MITRE ATT&CK T1059 — Command & Scripting" | LinkedIn, Twitter |
| How-to tutorial | "Build a YARA rule from scratch" | LinkedIn carousel |
| Mini case study | "How I caught a breach in 30 minutes" | LinkedIn, Twitter |
| Project walkthrough | "I built an open-source threat intel feed" | LinkedIn |
| "How I did it" | "My path from SOC analyst to DFIR lead" | LinkedIn, IG |
| Anonymized incident | "Anatomy of a ransomware attack (sanitized)" | All |

**Metrics that matter**: Comments, saves, DMs, profile visits

**Post frequency**: 30% of your content mix

**Funnel position**: Narrowing. Value density increases. Trust builds.

---

## BOFU — Bottom of Funnel (Decision)

**Goal**: Convert attention into opportunities (jobs, clients, collaborations).

**Audience**: Hot. Trusts you. Ready to act.

**Content types**:
| Type | Example | Platform |
|------|---------|----------|
| Portfolio showcase | "3 projects that prove I can detect APTs" | LinkedIn |
| "Hire me" post | "What I bring to your security team" | LinkedIn |
| ROI/business case | "How my detection rule saved $2M in breach costs" | LinkedIn |
| Success story | "From bootcamp to DFIR lead in 18 months" | LinkedIn, IG |
| Open-source release | "I built X, it's free, here's the repo" | Twitter, LinkedIn |
| Case study summary | "How I solved [specific problem]" | LinkedIn |
| CTA post | "I'm open to DFIR roles. DM me." | LinkedIn |

**Metrics that matter**: DMs, applications, offers, repo stars, collabs

**Post frequency**: 10% of your content mix

**Funnel position**: Narrow bottom. High intent. Clear CTA.

---

## The 70/20/10 Rule

- **70%** of posts: Educational value (TOFU + MOFU)
- **20%** of posts: Personal stories / behind-the-scenes
- **10%** of posts: Direct promotion (BOFU — hire me, buy, follow)

This ratio prevents audience fatigue while still converting.
